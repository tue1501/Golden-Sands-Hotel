Welcome to Golden Sands Hotel!

Nestled along one of Vietnam's most beautiful beaches, this resort promises a dream vacation and unforgettable experiences.

Immerse yourself in spacious surroundings with 285 modern rooms, each offering stunning views of the endless ocean. Enjoy premium facilities at our private 150-meter-long beach, along with round-the-clock entertainment and captivating shows at the G-Arena stage.

Relax by the infinity pool where the blues of the sky and sea meet, creating a romantic setting. Savor exquisite dishes and refined drinks at our seaside restaurants.

Golden Sands Hotel also takes pride in offering a world-class event space at G-Hall, perfect for meetings, conferences, or special events, tailored to your needs and style.

Recharge at Serenity Spa, inspired by the natural beauty of the ocean, or explore exciting cultural and recreational activities for the whole family.

Come to Golden Sands Hotel to enjoy pristine natural beauty alongside exceptional luxury. Our dedicated team is always ready to serve and provide you with the most complete experiences.
